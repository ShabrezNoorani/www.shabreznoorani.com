About Hi, I'm Shabrez

A Journey of Growth and Impact

Passionate and results-driven, I've navigated the complex worlds of operations consulting and account management with a keen eye for innovative solutions and a heart for making a difference. From enhancing mobile app campaign performances at Adikteev to pioneering social change with Ralith Milith, my journey is one of relentless pursuit of excellence and social good.

How I Got Here

With a Master's in Business Analysis and Consulting from IÉSEG and a bachelor's in Business Administration from Kashmir University, my foundation in business analysis was strong.
